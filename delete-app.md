# Account and Data Deletion

This page provides information about account and data deletion for our application.

## Delete Your Account

Users can permanently delete their account directly from within the app.

### Steps

1. Open the application.
2. Navigate to **Profile** or **Settings**.
3. Select **Delete Account**.
4. Confirm the deletion request.

## What Happens When You Delete Your Account?

When your account is deleted:

* Your account information is permanently removed.
* Your profile data is deleted.
* Associated user-generated data is removed from our systems where applicable.

## Data Retention

Certain information may be retained for a limited period if required for:

* Legal compliance
* Security purposes
* Fraud prevention

## Need Assistance?

If you experience any issues while deleting your account, please contact us through the support options available in the application.
